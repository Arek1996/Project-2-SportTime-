Why is worth to do sport

Sport improves mood

Physical exercise increases the production of the so-called happiness hormones - endorphins. They are formed in the pituitary gland. They are responsible for the joy and wide smile that can be seen on the faces of athletes even after exhausting competitions or training.

Increases learning abilities

One of the reasons why sport is worth doing is its effect on the nervous system. The brain is not only better supplied with blood and nourished. Physical activity increases the expression of the BDNF protein, which regulates the activity of neurotransmitters and enhances the reception of signals between nerve cells.

Playing sports increases self-esteem

The impact of sports on self-esteem is related not only to the improvement of the appearance of the figure. Thanks to him, faith in our own abilities increases, mood and well-being improve, thus we look at ourselves with a more favorable eye.

Increases the efficiency of the brain

Sport increases the expression of proteins responsible for the functioning of the nervous system - mainly the nervous tissue in the brain. It also promotes the multiplication of nerve cells, which has an impact on cognitive functions.

It reduces stress

Cortisol is responsible for stress. Regular, moderate exercise reduces its secretion. Thus, nervous tension is reduced. We also become more resistant to stressful situations in the future.

Sport is health

According to research, 30 minutes of regular exercise with moderate or high intensity is enough (but not overtraining because then the effect may be the opposite), which improve cognitive functioning (e.g. concentration, memory and learning ability). It also protects against memory deterioration and slows down dementia processes


Some basic details. For best results, exercise at moderate to high (but not too high) intensity for at least 30 minutes. Research has shown that a combination of aerobic (cardio) and anaerobic (strength) training can produce better results than aerobic exercise alone.
